How was Dice Wave Store started?

Five friends, Omra Al Shahed, Layan Al Alem, Yanal, Omar, and Nadeen, shared an unbridled love for board games. Their game nights, filled with laughter, strategy, and camaraderie, were the highlights of their week. As their collection of games grew, so did their desire to share this passion with others. Since 2015, this is the place we feel we share happiness with the world around us! To creating more memories and making friendships stronger!
